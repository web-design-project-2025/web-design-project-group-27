FALU SAUSAGE

Falukorv is a beloved Swedish comfort food made from a large, mild sausage typically baked or fried and often paired with potatoes or macaroni. Simple, hearty, and perfect for everyday meals.
30
4

What You’ll Need:

  • 1 ring of Falukorv (about 500g)
  • 2 tablespoons mustard
  • 100g cheese (sliced or shredded)
  • 1 onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 tablespoon butter or oil
  • Optional: ketchup for topping
  • Mashed potatoes or macaroni (for serving)

Just Follow These Steps:

  1. Preheat the oven to 200°C (390°F).
  2. Slice the sausage partially through, making cuts about 1cm apart.
  3. Insert cheese and onion slices into the cuts. Spread mustard on top.
  4. Place in a baking dish and drizzle with a bit of oil or dot with butter.
  5. Bake for 20–25 minutes, until the cheese is melted and the sausage is heated through.
  6. Serve warm with mashed potatoes or macaroni and enjoy!
