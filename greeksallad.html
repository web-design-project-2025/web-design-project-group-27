GREEK SALAD

Greek Salad is a refreshing and colorful dish made with ripe tomatoes, crisp cucumbers, red onions, olives, and feta cheese. It’s tossed with olive oil and herbs, making it a healthy and vibrant side or light meal.
15
4

What You’ll Need:

  • 3 ripe tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 cucumber, sliced
  • 1/2 red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1/2 cup Kalamata olives
  • 200g feta cheese, cubed or crumbled
  • 2 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 tbsp red wine vinegar (optional)
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Just Follow These Steps:

  1. In a large bowl, combine tomatoes, cucumber, onion, and olives.
  2. Add the feta cheese on top.
  3. Drizzle with olive oil and vinegar if using.
  4. Sprinkle with oregano, salt, and pepper.
  5. Toss gently to combine or leave as-is for a rustic presentation.
  6. Serve fresh as a side dish or light lunch.
