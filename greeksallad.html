GREEK SALAD

Greek Salad is a refreshing and healthy dish made with tomatoes, cucumbers, red onions, olives, and feta cheese, all tossed in a simple olive oil dressing. It’s a Mediterranean classic perfect as a side or light meal.

What You’ll Need:

  • 3 medium tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 cucumber, sliced
  • 1/2 red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1/2 cup Kalamata olives
  • 150g feta cheese, cubed or crumbled
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp red wine vinegar (optional)
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Just Follow These Steps:

  1. In a large bowl, combine tomatoes, cucumber, onion, and olives.
  2. Add feta cheese on top.
  3. Drizzle with olive oil and vinegar (if using).
  4. Sprinkle with oregano, salt, and pepper.
  5. Toss gently and serve chilled or at room temperature.
