PASTA BOLOGNESE

Pasta Bolognese is a hearty and classic Italian dish made with slow-cooked ground meat, tomatoes, and herbs, all tossed with your favorite pasta. It's comforting, rich, and perfect for a satisfying dinner.
45
4

What You'll Need:

  • 400g spaghetti or pasta of choice
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 carrot, finely grated
  • 500g ground beef or pork
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1 can (400g) chopped tomatoes
  • 1/2 tsp dried oregano
  • 1/2 tsp dried basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Parmesan cheese, for serving
  • Fresh basil (optional, for garnish)

Just Follow These Steps:

  1. Cook pasta according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. Heat olive oil in a large pan over medium heat. Add onions and garlic; sauté until soft.
  3. Add grated carrot and cook for another 2–3 minutes.
  4. Add ground meat and cook until browned.
  5. Stir in tomato paste, canned tomatoes, oregano, and basil. Simmer for 20–30 minutes.
  6. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  7. Toss the sauce with cooked pasta. Top with grated Parmesan and fresh basil.
