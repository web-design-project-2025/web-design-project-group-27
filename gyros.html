GYROS

Gyros is a classic Greek dish made with seasoned meat, often pork or chicken, cooked on a vertical rotisserie and served in warm pita with fresh vegetables and creamy tzatziki sauce.
60
4

What Youâ€™ll Need:

  • 500g pork shoulder or chicken thighs, thinly sliced
  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp red wine vinegar
  • 1 tsp oregano
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 4 pita breads
  • Sliced red onion, tomato, and lettuce
  • Tzatziki sauce (yogurt, cucumber, garlic, dill, lemon)

Just Follow These Steps:

  1. Mix olive oil, vinegar, garlic, oregano, paprika, salt, and pepper. Marinate meat for at least 30 minutes.
  2. Grill or pan-fry meat until golden and cooked through.
  3. Warm pita bread on a skillet or in the oven.
  4. Assemble gyros by layering meat, vegetables, and tzatziki onto the pita.
  5. Fold and wrap in parchment or foil for easy eating.
  6. Serve immediately with fries or a Greek salad on the side.
