SWEDISH MEATBALLS

Swedish meatballs, or köttbullar, are a classic comfort food made with a blend of ground beef and pork, gently spiced and served with creamy gravy, mashed potatoes, and lingonberry jam.
45
4

What You’ll Need:

  • 250g ground beef
  • 250g ground pork
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 1 egg
  • 100ml milk
  • 50g breadcrumbs
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/4 tsp allspice (optional)
  • Butter and oil for frying
  • Mashed potatoes and lingonberry jam (for serving)

Just Follow These Steps:

  1. Soak breadcrumbs in milk for 5 minutes. Add egg and mix.
  2. Combine beef, pork, onion, soaked breadcrumbs, and spices. Mix thoroughly.
  3. Form small meatballs and let rest in the fridge for 15 minutes.
  4. Fry meatballs in butter and oil until browned and cooked through.
  5. Optional: Make a cream sauce using the pan drippings, flour, beef stock, and cream.
  6. Serve warm with mashed potatoes, lingonberry jam, and pickled cucumber.
