KALOPS

Kalops is a classic Swedish beef stew, slow-cooked with onions, carrots, and allspice. This hearty dish is perfect for cold days and traditionally served with boiled potatoes and pickled beets.
120
4

What Youâ€™ll Need:

  • 800g stewing beef, cubed
  • 1 large onion, sliced
  • 2 carrots, sliced
  • 2 tablespoons flour
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 3 cups beef stock
  • 5 whole allspice
  • 2 bay leaves
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Boiled potatoes and pickled beets (for serving)

Just Follow These Steps:

  1. In a large pot, brown the beef cubes in butter.
  2. Add flour to coat the meat and cook for another minute.
  3. Add sliced onions, carrots, beef stock, allspice, and bay leaves.
  4. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and let simmer for 1.5 to 2 hours until the meat is tender.
  5. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  6. Serve hot with boiled potatoes and pickled beets on the side.
