BEEF AND BROCCOLI

Beef and Broccoli is a classic stir-fry dish made with tender beef slices, fresh broccoli, and a savory garlic soy sauce. It's quick, flavorful, and perfect served with steamed rice.
45
4

What You’ll Need:

  • 1/2 lb flank steak, thinly sliced
  • 2 cups broccoli florets
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp oyster sauce
  • 1 tsp cornstarch
  • 1 garlic clove, minced
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Just Follow These Steps:

  1. Marinate beef with soy sauce, oyster sauce, and cornstarch for 15 minutes.
  2. Blanch broccoli in boiling water for 1–2 minutes, then set aside.
  3. Heat oil in a pan, cook garlic until fragrant.
  4. Add beef and cook until browned.
  5. Add broccoli and stir-fry for 2–3 minutes. Season with salt and pepper.
