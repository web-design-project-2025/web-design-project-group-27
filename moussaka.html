MOUSSAKA

Moussaka is a traditional Greek casserole layered with sautéed eggplant, spiced ground meat, and a creamy béchamel sauce. Rich, hearty, and comforting—this dish is a Mediterranean favorite perfect for family meals.
45
4

What You’ll Need:

  • 2 eggplants, sliced and salted
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 500g ground beef or lamb
  • 1 can diced tomatoes
  • 1/2 tsp cinnamon
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • For Béchamel Sauce:
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 2 tbsp flour
  • 2 cups milk
  • 1 egg yolk
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan

Just Follow These Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C). Pat eggplant slices dry and lightly fry or bake them.
  2. Sauté onion and garlic in olive oil. Add ground meat and cook until browned.
  3. Stir in tomatoes, cinnamon, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 15–20 minutes.
  4. In a saucepan, melt butter, whisk in flour, then gradually add milk to make béchamel. Cook until thickened.
  5. Remove from heat and stir in egg yolk and Parmesan.
  6. In a baking dish, layer eggplant, meat sauce, and top with béchamel.
  7. Bake for 40–45 minutes until golden and bubbling. Let rest before serving.
