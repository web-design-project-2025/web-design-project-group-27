CHICKEN TIKKA MASALA

Chicken Tikka Masala features marinated and grilled chicken pieces simmered in a creamy, spiced tomato-based curry. This rich, flavorful dish is a favorite worldwide and pairs perfectly with naan or rice.
45
4

What You’ll Need:

  • 500g boneless chicken, cubed
  • 1/2 cup yogurt
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp turmeric
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp chili powder
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, pureed
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander for garnish

Just Follow These Steps:

  1. Marinate chicken in yogurt, lemon juice, ginger-garlic paste, turmeric, garam masala, and chili powder for at least 1 hour.
  2. Grill or pan-fry the marinated chicken until cooked and slightly charred. Set aside.
  3. In a pan, heat oil and sauté onions until soft.
  4. Add pureed tomatoes and cumin. Cook until the sauce thickens and oil separates.
  5. Stir in cream and season with salt. Simmer for 5 minutes.
  6. Add grilled chicken to the sauce and simmer for another 10 minutes.
  7. Garnish with coriander and serve with rice or naan.
