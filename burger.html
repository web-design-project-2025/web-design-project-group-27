CLASSIC BURGER

The classic burger is a juicy, flavorful grilled beef patty nestled in a soft bun and topped with melty cheese, fresh vegetables, and your favorite condiments. It's a timeless American comfort food that never goes out of style.

What You’ll Need:

  • 500g ground beef (80/20 blend)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 4 burger buns
  • 4 slices cheddar cheese
  • Lettuce leaves
  • Tomato slices
  • Red onion slices
  • Pickles (optional)
  • Ketchup, mustard, or mayonnaise

Just Follow These Steps:

  1. Form ground beef into 4 equal patties and season both sides with salt and pepper.
  2. Preheat a grill or skillet to medium-high heat. Cook patties for 3–4 minutes per side or until desired doneness.
  3. Add a slice of cheese to each patty during the last minute of cooking.
  4. Lightly toast burger buns on the grill or pan.
  5. Assemble burgers with lettuce, tomato, onion, and pickles.
  6. Add condiments and top with the bun. Serve hot with fries or chips.
