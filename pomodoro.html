POMODORO PASTA

Pomodoro Pasta is a simple, yet incredibly flavorful Italian dish made with pasta, fresh tomatoes, garlic, olive oil, and basil. It’s a perfect meal when you want something quick, fresh, and full of summer flavors.
45
4

What You’ll Need:

  • 400g spaghetti or your favorite pasta
  • 4 ripe tomatoes, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1/4 cup fresh basil, chopped
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste
  • Parmesan cheese, grated (optional)
  • 1/4 tsp red pepper flakes (optional)

Just Follow These Steps:

  1. Cook the pasta in salted boiling water according to package instructions until al dente. Drain, reserving 1 cup of pasta water.
  2. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and sauté for about 1 minute, until fragrant.
  3. Add the chopped tomatoes to the skillet and cook for 5–7 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the tomatoes break down into a sauce.
  4. Season the sauce with salt, pepper, and red pepper flakes, if using. Add the cooked pasta to the skillet and toss to coat, adding reserved pasta water as needed to reach desired consistency.
  5. Stir in the fresh basil and cook for another minute to combine.
  6. Serve immediately, topped with freshly grated Parmesan cheese if desired.
