PALAK PANEER

Palak Paneer is a classic North Indian dish made with soft paneer (Indian cheese) cubes simmered in a vibrant spinach gravy spiced with garlic, ginger, and garam masala. It's rich, nutritious, and best served with naan or jeera rice.
45
4

What You’ll Need:

  • 250g paneer, cubed
  • 300g spinach leaves (palak)
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 1 tsp ginger, grated
  • 1 green chili (optional)
  • 1/2 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric
  • 1/2 tsp garam masala
  • 2 tbsp cream or yogurt (optional)
  • 2 tbsp oil or ghee
  • Salt to taste

Just Follow These Steps:

  1. Blanch spinach leaves in hot water for 2–3 minutes, then transfer to cold water. Blend into a smooth puree with garlic, ginger, and green chili.
  2. Heat oil in a pan, add cumin seeds and sauté onions until golden.
  3. Add chopped tomatoes and cook until soft. Add turmeric and salt.
  4. Stir in the spinach puree and cook for 5–7 minutes on medium heat.
  5. Add paneer cubes and simmer gently for another 5 minutes.
  6. Sprinkle garam masala and mix in cream or yogurt if using.
  7. Serve hot with naan or rice.
