CHICKEN KORMA

Chicken Korma is a luxurious Indian curry made with tender chicken simmered in a creamy, mildly spiced sauce rich in yogurt, cream, nuts, and aromatic spices. A perfect dish for special occasions or a comforting meal.
45
4

What You’ll Need:

  • 500g chicken, cut into pieces
  • 1/2 cup plain yogurt
  • 1/4 cup cream or coconut milk
  • 2 onions, finely sliced
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 tsp ginger, grated
  • 1/4 cup ground almonds or cashews
  • 2 tbsp oil or ghee
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 1/2 tsp cumin
  • 1/2 tsp garam masala
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander for garnish

Just Follow These Steps:

  1. Marinate chicken in yogurt, turmeric, and salt for at least 30 minutes.
  2. Heat oil in a pan and fry sliced onions until golden. Remove half for garnish.
  3. Add garlic, ginger, and ground nuts to the pan. Sauté briefly.
  4. Add marinated chicken and cook until lightly browned.
  5. Stir in coriander, cumin, and a splash of water. Cover and simmer for 15–20 minutes.
  6. Add cream and garam masala. Cook another 5 minutes until thick and rich.
  7. Top with fried onions and fresh coriander. Serve with rice or naan.
