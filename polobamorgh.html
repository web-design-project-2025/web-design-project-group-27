POLO BA MORGH

Polo ba Morgh is a classic Persian dish combining tender chicken simmered in a rich tomato-saffron sauce and served with fluffy basmati rice. The dish is fragrant, colorful, and perfect for festive dinners or special gatherings. It’s often accompanied by yogurt, pickles, or a simple side salad.
45
4

What You’ll Need:

  • 4 chicken drumsticks or thighs
  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 1 large onion (chopped)
  • 3 tbsp tomato paste
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric
  • 1/2 tsp cinnamon (optional)
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • 1/2 tsp saffron (dissolved in hot water)
  • 2-3 cups water or chicken broth

Just Follow These Steps:

  1. Season chicken with salt, pepper, and turmeric. In a large pan, heat oil and sear the chicken on both sides until golden.
  2. Remove chicken and set aside. In the same pan, sauté onions until golden. Add tomato paste and cook for 2-3 minutes.
  3. Return the chicken to the pan. Add water or broth. Simmer on low heat for 45-60 minutes, until the chicken is fully cooked and the sauce thickens.
  4. Meanwhile, rinse and soak the basmati rice for 30 minutes. Bring a pot of water to boil, then cook the rice until al dente. Drain.
  5. In a large pot, layer the rice and chicken mixture. Pour saffron water on top.
  6. Cover with a clean towel and lid. Steam on low heat for 30-40 minutes until the rice is fully cooked.
  7. Gently fluff and serve the rice and chicken together. Garnish with extra saffron or barberries if desired.
