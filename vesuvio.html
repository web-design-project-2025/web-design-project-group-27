VESUVIO PIZZA

Vesuvio Pizza is an Italian pizza with a fiery twist. This pizza is topped with spicy pepperoni, smoked sausage, and a zesty marinara sauce, providing the perfect balance of heat and flavor. It's a bold and satisfying dish for spice lovers.
45
4

What You’ll Need:

  • 1 pizza dough (homemade or store-bought)
  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce
  • 1 1/2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup sliced pepperoni
  • 1/2 cup smoked sausage, sliced
  • 1/2 red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 tsp red pepper flakes (for extra heat)
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Fresh basil leaves (optional for garnish)
  • Flour for dusting

Just Follow These Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 475°F (245°C) and place a pizza stone or baking tray inside to heat.
  2. Roll out pizza dough on a floured surface to your desired thickness.
  3. Spread pizza sauce evenly over the dough.
  4. Top the sauce with shredded mozzarella cheese, followed by the pepperoni, smoked sausage, and red onion slices.
  5. Sprinkle red pepper flakes on top for added heat, and drizzle olive oil over the pizza.
  6. Transfer the pizza to the preheated stone or tray and bake for 10–12 minutes, or until the crust is golden and cheese is bubbling.
  7. Once baked, remove from the oven and garnish with fresh basil leaves if desired.
  8. Slice and enjoy your fiery Vesuvio Pizza!
