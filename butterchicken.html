BUTTER CHICKEN

Butter Chicken, or Murgh Makhani, is a beloved Indian dish featuring tender chicken simmered in a creamy tomato-based sauce infused with aromatic spices. It's rich, flavorful, and perfect served with naan or basmati rice.
45
4

What You'll Need:

  • 500g boneless chicken, cubed
  • 1 cup plain yogurt
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp turmeric
  • 1 tsp chili powder
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cups tomato purée
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander for garnish

Just Follow These Steps:

  1. Marinate chicken in yogurt, lemon juice, ginger-garlic paste, turmeric, chili powder, and garam masala for at least 1 hour.
  2. Heat butter in a pan and sauté onions until golden.
  3. Add marinated chicken and cook until browned.
  4. Stir in tomato purée and simmer for 15-20 minutes.
  5. Pour in cream, season with salt, and simmer another 5-10 minutes.
  6. Garnish with fresh coriander and serve with naan or rice.
