GHEYMEH BADEMJAN

Gheymeh Bademjan is a popular Persian stew made with tender lamb or beef, split peas, and fried eggplant in a rich tomato-based sauce. It's aromatic with a touch of cinnamon and turmeric, and often served with fluffy saffron rice. This dish offers a perfect blend of savory, tangy, and comforting flavors.
45
4

What You’ll Need:

  • 500g lamb or beef, cut into cubes
  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup split peas
  • 2 large eggplants, sliced into rounds
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped or 1 can of diced tomatoes
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1 tsp turmeric
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
  • 1/2 tsp cumin
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 3 cups water or beef broth
  • Saffron rice for serving

Just Follow These Steps:

  1. In a large pot, heat some olive oil over medium heat and sauté the chopped onions and garlic until golden brown.
  2. Add the cubed lamb or beef to the pot and brown on all sides. Season with turmeric, cinnamon, cumin, salt, and pepper.
  3. Add the chopped tomatoes (or canned tomatoes) and tomato paste. Stir and cook for a few minutes to combine the flavors.
  4. Pour in 3 cups of water or beef broth and bring to a boil. Reduce the heat, cover, and let simmer for about 1 hour, or until the meat is tender.
  5. In the meantime, fry the eggplant slices in olive oil until golden and crispy. Set aside on a paper towel to drain excess oil.
  6. Add the split peas to the stew and continue cooking for another 30 minutes until the peas are tender and the stew thickens.
  7. Once the meat, peas, and sauce are ready, gently stir in the fried eggplant slices. Let them simmer for 10 more minutes to absorb the flavors.
  8. Serve Gheymeh Bademjan hot with saffron rice. Garnish with additional fried onions or herbs if desired.
