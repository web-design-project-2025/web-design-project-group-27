PEPPERONI PIZZA

Pepperoni Pizza is a timeless favorite topped with zesty tomato sauce, melted mozzarella cheese, and slices of spicy pepperoni. Perfectly baked until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly, it’s a go-to for any pizza night.
45
4

What You’ll Need:

  • 1 pizza dough (homemade or store-bought)
  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce
  • 1 1/2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup sliced pepperoni
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1/2 tsp dried oregano
  • 1/4 tsp red pepper flakes (optional)
  • Flour for dusting

Just Follow These Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 475°F (245°C) and place a pizza stone or baking tray inside to heat.
  2. Roll out pizza dough on a floured surface to your desired thickness.
  3. Spread pizza sauce evenly over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges.
  4. Sprinkle mozzarella cheese on top, then arrange the pepperoni slices evenly.
  5. Drizzle with olive oil and sprinkle oregano and red pepper flakes if using.
  6. Transfer to the preheated tray or stone and bake for 10–12 minutes, until crust is golden and cheese is bubbly.
  7. Remove from oven, let cool slightly, slice, and enjoy!
