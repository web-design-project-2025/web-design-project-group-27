FRIED RICE

Chicken Fried Rice is a savory dish made with stir-fried rice, chicken, vegetables, and soy sauce. It's flavorful, quick to make, and perfect for a satisfying meal.

What You’ll Need:

  • 2 cups cooked rice
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 garlic clove, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Just Follow These Steps:

  1. Heat oil in a pan and scramble the eggs. Set aside.
  2. Add garlic and vegetables, sauté for 2–3 minutes.
  3. Add the rice and mix well.
  4. Add soy sauce, eggs, salt, and pepper.
  5. Stir-fry everything together for 3–5 minutes.
