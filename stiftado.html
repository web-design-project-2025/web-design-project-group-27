STIFADO

Stifado is a slow-cooked Greek beef stew rich with pearl onions, red wine, and fragrant spices. This hearty dish is perfect for cold days and best enjoyed with crusty bread or over rice.
45
4

What You’ll Need:

  • 800g beef, cut into chunks
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 500g pearl onions, peeled
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 cup red wine
  • 1 can chopped tomatoes
  • 2 tbsp red wine vinegar
  • 1 cinnamon stick
  • 2–3 cloves
  • 1 bay leaf
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Just Follow These Steps:

  1. Heat olive oil in a large pot. Brown the beef in batches and set aside.
  2. In the same pot, sauté pearl onions until lightly golden.
  3. Add garlic and cook briefly, then return the beef to the pot.
  4. Stir in wine, vinegar, tomatoes, and spices. Season with salt and pepper.
  5. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat to low. Cover and simmer for 1.5–2 hours until tender.
  6. Remove the lid for the last 20 minutes to let the sauce thicken slightly.
  7. Serve hot with bread, mashed potatoes, or rice.
