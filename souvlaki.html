SOUVLAKI

Souvlaki is a beloved Greek street food made with skewered and grilled marinated meat—often served with warm pita, fresh vegetables, and a tangy tzatziki sauce. It’s flavorful, portable, and perfect for any occasion.

What You’ll Need:

  • 500g pork or chicken, cut into cubes
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Wooden skewers, soaked
  • Optional: pita bread, sliced onions, tomatoes, tzatziki

Just Follow These Steps:

  1. In a bowl, combine olive oil, lemon juice, garlic, oregano, salt, and pepper.
  2. Add the meat cubes and marinate for at least 1 hour (preferably overnight).
  3. Thread the marinated meat onto soaked skewers.
  4. Grill over medium-high heat for 10–12 minutes, turning occasionally, until cooked through.
  5. Serve hot with pita bread, fresh veggies, and a side of tzatziki sauce.
