TACOS

A beloved Mexican street food, tacos feature warm tortillas filled with savory meat, fresh toppings, and bold spices. Versatile and easy to customize, tacos are perfect for quick dinners, parties, or taco night!
45
4

What You’ll Need:

  • 500g ground beef or chicken
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 packet taco seasoning (or homemade)
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 8 small corn or flour tortillas
  • Shredded lettuce
  • Diced tomatoes
  • Grated cheese
  • Sour cream or Greek yogurt
  • Salsa or hot sauce
  • Fresh cilantro and lime wedges (optional)

Just Follow These Steps:

  1. Heat olive oil in a pan over medium heat. Sauté onion and garlic until soft.
  2. Add ground meat and cook until browned.
  3. Stir in taco seasoning and water. Simmer for 5–7 minutes until thickened.
  4. Warm tortillas in a skillet or microwave.
  5. Fill each tortilla with meat and your favorite toppings.
  6. Serve immediately with lime wedges and hot sauce if desired.
