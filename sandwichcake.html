SANDWICH CAKE

Smörgåstårta is a savory Swedish cake made with layers of bread and flavorful fillings like shrimp, salmon, ham, egg, and creamy spreads. It’s decorated like a cake but eaten like a sandwich — perfect for celebrations and impressing guests!
60
6

What You’ll Need:

  • 12 slices white or rye sandwich bread, crusts removed
  • 200g cooked shrimp, chopped
  • 200g smoked salmon, sliced
  • 100g ham, chopped
  • 3 hard-boiled eggs, chopped
  • 1 cup crème fraîche or sour cream
  • 1 cup mayonnaise
  • 1 tbsp Dijon mustard
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Lettuce, cucumber, cherry tomatoes, dill (for decoration)

Just Follow These Steps:

  1. Mix shrimp with mayonnaise and a bit of mustard. In a separate bowl, mix ham and eggs with crème fraîche.
  2. Lay down the first layer of bread, then spread the shrimp mixture evenly.
  3. Add another bread layer, then spread the ham and egg mix.
  4. Add a final layer of bread, then cover the entire cake with a mixture of mayonnaise and sour cream.
  5. Decorate the cake with smoked salmon, shrimp, cucumber slices, halved cherry tomatoes, lettuce, and fresh dill.
  6. Chill for at least 1 hour before serving for best flavor.
  7. Slice like a cake and enjoy your savory Swedish masterpiece!
