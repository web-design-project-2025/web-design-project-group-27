CLASSIC CASSEROLE

This classic casserole is the ultimate comfort food—a creamy, cheesy, oven-baked dish loaded with hearty ingredients like ground beef, pasta, and vegetables. Perfect for feeding a crowd or preparing ahead for busy nights.
45
4

What You’ll Need:

  • 500g ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 can diced tomatoes
  • 1 cup tomato sauce
  • 1 cup cooked pasta (e.g., elbow macaroni)
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1/2 cup sour cream
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional: parsley or green onions for garnish

Just Follow These Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C). Grease a baking dish.
  2. In a skillet, cook ground beef with chopped onion until browned. Add garlic and sauté briefly.
  3. Stir in diced tomatoes and tomato sauce. Simmer for 10 minutes.
  4. Mix in cooked pasta and sour cream. Season with salt and pepper.
  5. Pour mixture into the baking dish and top with shredded cheese.
  6. Bake for 20–25 minutes or until bubbly and golden on top.
  7. Let rest a few minutes before serving. Garnish as desired.
