CALZONE

Calzone is a folded Italian pizza stuffed with a delicious combination of cheese, meats, and vegetables. Baked until golden and crispy, it's a satisfying meal that’s easy to customize and perfect for dipping in marinara sauce.
45
4

What You’ll Need:

  • 1 pizza dough (homemade or store-bought)
  • 1/2 cup marinara sauce
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup ricotta cheese
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan
  • 1/2 cup cooked Italian sausage or pepperoni (optional)
  • 1/4 cup sautéed vegetables (peppers, onions, mushrooms)
  • 1 egg (for egg wash)
  • Flour for dusting
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Just Follow These Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 220°C (425°F) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. Roll out the pizza dough into a circle on a floured surface.
  3. Spread a thin layer of marinara sauce on one half of the dough, leaving edges clear.
  4. Top with mozzarella, ricotta, Parmesan, meats, and veggies.
  5. Fold the dough over to create a half-moon shape and crimp the edges tightly.
  6. Brush the top with beaten egg for a golden crust.
  7. Bake for 15–20 minutes or until golden brown and cooked through.
  8. Let cool slightly before slicing. Serve with extra marinara sauce.
