MAC AND CHEESE

Creamy, cheesy, and irresistibly comforting, this classic mac and cheese is a staple in American kitchens. Made with a rich cheese sauce and baked to golden perfection, it’s perfect as a side or a hearty main dish.
45
4

What You’ll Need:

  • 250g elbow macaroni
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 2 tbsp flour
  • 2 cups milk
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper
  • 1/4 tsp mustard powder (optional)
  • 2 cups shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1/2 cup breadcrumbs (optional, for topping)

Just Follow These Steps:

  1. Cook macaroni according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a saucepan, melt butter over medium heat. Stir in flour to make a roux.
  3. Slowly whisk in milk. Cook until thickened, about 5–7 minutes.
  4. Season with salt, pepper, and mustard powder if using.
  5. Remove from heat and stir in shredded cheese until melted.
  6. Combine cheese sauce with cooked pasta and mix well.
  7. Pour into a greased baking dish. Top with breadcrumbs and bake at 375°F (190°C) for 20–25 minutes, until golden.
