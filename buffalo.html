BUFFALO WINGS

Buffalo wings are a beloved American classic—crispy chicken wings tossed in a tangy, spicy hot sauce. Perfect for game day or casual gatherings, these wings are best served with celery sticks and blue cheese dip.
45
4

What You'll Need:

  • 1 kg chicken wings, separated
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 1/2 cup hot sauce (e.g., Frank's RedHot)
  • 2 tbsp melted butter
  • Optional: celery sticks & blue cheese dressing

Just Follow These Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 425°F (220°C). Line a baking tray with foil and a wire rack.
  2. Pat wings dry and toss with oil, salt, pepper, and garlic powder.
  3. Arrange wings on the rack and bake for 40–45 minutes, flipping halfway through.
  4. In a small saucepan, combine hot sauce and melted butter. Warm through.
  5. Toss baked wings in the sauce until fully coated.
  6. Serve immediately with celery and blue cheese or ranch dip.
