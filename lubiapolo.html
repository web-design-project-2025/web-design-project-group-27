LUBIA POLO

Lubia Polo is a comforting Persian rice dish layered with spiced ground beef, green beans, and aromatic tomato sauce. It’s a hearty, one-pot meal that's perfect for weeknight dinners and family gatherings. Traditionally served with yogurt or salad shirazi, Lubia Polo offers a beautiful blend of flavors and textures in every bite.
45
4

What You’ll Need:

  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 300g ground beef or lamb
  • 2 cups green beans (chopped)
  • 1 medium onion (chopped)
  • 3 tbsp tomato paste
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1/2 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • Salt to taste
  • 1/2 tsp saffron (dissolved in hot water)
  • Optional: 1 medium tomato (diced)

Just Follow These Steps:

  1. Wash the rice and soak in salted water for at least 30 minutes. Drain before cooking.
  2. In a large pan, heat 1 tbsp of oil and sauté chopped onions until golden. Add ground beef and cook until browned.
  3. Stir in turmeric, black pepper, cinnamon, and salt. Add tomato paste and cook for 2-3 minutes until well combined.
  4. Add the chopped green beans (and optional diced tomato) to the pan. Cook for 10-15 minutes until beans are tender.
  5. In a separate pot, bring water to a boil. Add rice and cook until just al dente (about 5-6 minutes). Drain.
  6. In a large pot, layer the rice and meat mixture. Start and end with rice. Drizzle saffron water over the top.
  7. Cover with a clean towel and lid. Steam on low heat for 30-40 minutes until the rice is fully cooked and fluffy.
  8. Fluff the rice gently, serve hot with yogurt or salad shirazi.
