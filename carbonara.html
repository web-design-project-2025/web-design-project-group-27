SPAGHETTI CARBONARA

Spaghetti Carbonara is a Roman classic made with pasta, eggs, Pecorino Romano cheese, pancetta, and black pepper. It’s creamy without cream and relies on timing and technique for a rich, comforting dish.
45
4

What You’ll Need:

  • 400g spaghetti
  • 150g pancetta or guanciale, diced
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 cup grated Pecorino Romano cheese
  • Freshly ground black pepper
  • Salt for pasta water

Just Follow These Steps:

  1. Bring salted water to a boil and cook spaghetti until al dente. Reserve 1 cup of pasta water before draining.
  2. In a bowl, whisk together eggs, grated cheese, and plenty of black pepper.
  3. In a pan, cook pancetta over medium heat until crispy. Remove from heat.
  4. Add hot, drained pasta to the pan and toss with pancetta.
  5. Slowly add egg mixture, stirring quickly to create a creamy sauce. Add reserved pasta water as needed.
  6. Serve immediately, topped with extra cheese and pepper.
