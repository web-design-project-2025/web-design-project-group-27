NASI GORENG

Nasi Goreng is a flavorful Indonesian fried rice dish made with cooked rice, soy sauce, garlic, shallots, and a mix of vegetables, often topped with a fried egg. It’s quick, spicy, and deeply satisfying.
45
4

What You’ll Need:

  • 2 cups cooked rice (preferably cold)
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 shallots, finely chopped
  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 cup mixed vegetables (peas, carrots, etc.)
  • 2 tbsp sweet soy sauce (kecap manis)
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tsp chili paste (optional)
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Just Follow These Steps:

  1. Heat oil in a pan and sauté garlic and shallots until fragrant.
  2. Add vegetables and stir-fry for 2–3 minutes.
  3. Push veggies aside and scramble the egg in the pan.
  4. Add rice, soy sauce, sweet soy sauce, and chili paste. Stir well.
  5. Cook for 3–5 minutes until heated through. Season to taste.
  6. Serve hot with a fried egg on top (optional).
