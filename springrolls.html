SPRING ROLLS

Spring Rolls are crispy appetizers filled with a mixture of vegetables and sometimes meat, wrapped in a thin pastry and deep fried. They are flavorful, crunchy, and perfect as a snack or starter.
45
4

What You’ll Need:

  • 10 spring roll wrappers
  • 1 cup shredded cabbage
  • 1/2 cup grated carrot
  • 1/2 cup bean sprouts
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tsp sesame oil
  • 1 garlic clove, minced
  • Oil for frying

Just Follow These Steps:

  1. Heat sesame oil and sauté garlic and vegetables for 2–3 minutes.
  2. Add soy sauce and stir well. Let filling cool.
  3. Place 1–2 tbsp of filling on a wrapper, fold and roll tightly.
  4. Seal edge with water or flour paste.
  5. Deep fry in hot oil until golden brown.
