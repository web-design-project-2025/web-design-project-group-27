PAD THAI

Pad Thai is a popular Thai stir-fried noodle dish made with rice noodles, shrimp or chicken, tofu, peanuts, scrambled egg, and bean sprouts. It’s tossed in a sweet-savory-tangy tamarind sauce for a perfectly balanced flavor.

Ingredients

  • 200g rice noodles
  • 2 tbsp tamarind paste
  • 2 tbsp fish sauce
  • 1 tbsp brown sugar
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 egg
  • 100g shrimp or chicken, sliced
  • 1/2 cup bean sprouts
  • 1/4 cup chopped green onions
  • 2 tbsp crushed peanuts
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • Lime wedges for serving

Instructions

  1. Soak rice noodles in warm water until soft. Drain and set aside.
  2. Mix tamarind paste, fish sauce, and brown sugar in a bowl to create the sauce.
  3. Heat oil in a pan. Sauté garlic, then add shrimp or chicken and cook until done.
  4. Push aside the protein and scramble the egg in the pan.
  5. Add noodles and sauce. Stir-fry until evenly coated.
  6. Stir in bean sprouts and green onions. Cook briefly.
  7. Serve hot with crushed peanuts and lime wedges.
